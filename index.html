About Us

Graduate Student Association in Computing, aka. GSAC, is a student-led body within the NUS School of Computing that strives to better the experience of graduate students by organising events that benefit their overall well-being while furthering their professional life. GSAC aims to achieve this by regularly organising fun social events and sharing resources for studentsâ€™ career development. Currently, GSAC is run by a team of six enthusiastic graduate students, and is advised by Prof. Kuldeep Meel.

If you are willing to help organise GSAC events, please reach out to us through SoC slack.

WE AIM TO IMPROVE GRADUATE LIFE

One of our central goals is to help the department (SoC) understand changing student needs and well-being.

We aim to create a channel for disseminating information regarding internship and full-time job opportunities for students.

Organise research talks as well as alumni talks to promote research collaboration and industry involvement.

Create several sub-groups for different fun activities, and conduct weekly events to relieve stress.

Offer blogs on topics that SoC graduate students will find relevant and useful.
